# Integrations

Any literature tool can connect to Article Galaxy using OpenURL. Follow the instructions below to connect your tools now.

# ChemPort

Enables you to send requests for articles not available in your ejournal subscription base to Reprints Desk right from the ChemPort interface.

# Setup

Please contact your company’s ChemPort administrator or Get Help from Reprints Desk customer support.

# EBSCOhost

Seamlessly connect from EBSCOHost to Reprints Desk when looking for an article not available in your EbscoHOST subscriptions.

# Setup

Please contact your company’s EBSCOhost administrator or Get Help from Reprints Desk customer support.

# Embase

Articles not available as full-text in Embase can now be requested form Reprints Desk directly within the Embase interface. With a click of a button, you’ll be able to order the full text pronto.

# Setup

Please contact your company’s Embase administrator or Get Help from Reprints Desk customer support.

# FIZ AutoDoc

FIZ AutoDoc is a paid service that allows you and other users to order via the FIZ AutoDoc platform, then analyzes your order and forwards it to Reprints Desk or any other document delivery supplier that is selected based on your unique ordering criteria.

# Setup

Contact FIZ Karlsruhe by phone at +49 7247 808 555 or by e-mail: helpdesk@fiz-karlsruhe.de

# Setup

Visit IEEE Xplore online at http://www.ieee.org/publications_standards/publications/subscriptions/clientservices/openurl.html

# OvidSP

OvidSP is the a leading information search, discovery, and management solution that provides professionals in science, medicine, and healthcare all over the world with a single online destination for seamlessly accessing and working with premium online journals, books, and databases from the world’s leading publishers.

OvidSP is designed to save you time and maximize your research productivity, by offering: a variety of search styles; an intuitive interface; customizable and flexible work productivity tools, a dedicated work area for managing and organizing critical research findings; and unique add-on tools for extending your research activity beyond OvidSP. That means you can spend more time focused on your research, stay ahead of advancements in your field, and work the way you want to work.

Reprints Desk integrates with OvidSP to save time submitting orders for full-text articles that aren’t available through paid e-resources on the OvidSP platform. An icon will appear in the OvidSP user interface (UI) that you and other users can click to initiate your document delivery request.

# Setup

Please contact your company’s OvidSP administrator or Get Help from Reprints Desk customer support.

# QUOSA

QUOSA DocFlow (Integrated Document Delivery) gives you and other users seamless and immediate access to your collection of journal articles acquired through document delivery and makes ordering articles via document delivery quick and painless.

DocFlow works in conjunction with the Virtual Library to provide the following functionality with the main goal of eliminating duplicate document delivery orders:

Users searching for journal articles outside of QUOSA (e.g. using a Web browser to search in OvidSP, PubMed, Embase, Scopus-wherever your link resolver is integrated) see a “get document” link associated with hits in the results set. When users click this link, DocFlow will check the Virtual Library and present available articles directly to the user. If the desired article is not in the Virtual Library, QUOSA will complete the document delivery order form with the corresponding citation information so that the article may be ordered. DocFlow is integrated with Reprints Desk to submit orders automatically. When these orders are filled, DocFlow will retrieve the articles from the document delivery vendor and publish them directly into the Virtual Library in a manner compliant with the article’s copyright. QUOSA Information Manager users may also order documents from Information Express Online and Reprints Desk from within QUOSA. These orders will also be delivered directly to the Virtual Library for immediate access via Virtual Library Plus. DocFlow Integrated Document Delivery manages the document delivery process directly within the article search workflow. It helps you keep all articles acquired through document delivery in one place to eliminate duplicate orders and ensures that article seekers have immediate access to a complete article collection.

# Setup

Please contact your company’s QUOSA administrator or Get Help from Reprints Desk customer support.

# RefMan

Connecting RefMan with Article Galaxy enables you to send orders for full-text articles from within RefManto Reprints Desk.

# Setup

The following one-time setup is required for each RefMan installation:

Click Tools then Options (or press F11) Click the Internet Search tab. Select Enable OpenURL For the OpenURL path type: http://www.reprintsdesk.com/landing/po.aspx Leave the Arguments box as is. Click on OK.

# Use

To link from a Refman citation to a prepopulated Reprints Desk form:

In RefMan, click on the desired citation or double click it to view the full record. Click on References then OpenURL Record Link (or) right click with mouse and choose OpenURL Record Link.

Save time and hassle ordering full-text articles from within Scopus with seamless integration to Reprints Desk so you and other users can send article requests directly from Scopus to Reprints Desk.
